Founded in 1919, Clermont School of Business (École de Management de Clermont) is a leading French Grande École offering highly ranked business programs. The school is renowned for its international focus, close links with corporate partners, and strong commitment to student employability and entrepreneurial development. With campuses in Clermont-Ferrand and Paris, and a global network of 50+ partner universities, it offers students a truly global education experience.

Campuses & Locations
Clermont-Ferrand (Main Campus) Located in the heart of Clermont-Ferrand, a vibrant and student-friendly city in central France, surrounded by the stunning Auvergne volcanoes. The campus offers modern learning facilities, student co-working spaces, innovation incubators, and dedicated international student support. Close to major transport links with direct TGV trains to Paris (3 hours).
Paris Campus The school maintains a Parisian base specifically tailored for advanced postgraduate programs, work-study tracks, and networking events with major multinational corporations. Strategically located near La Défense business district, providing students direct access to a vast employer and alumni network.

General Admission
Required Academic Credentials High school diploma or equivalent (Baccalaureate) for undergraduate admission; Bachelor's degree from an accredited university for graduate admission. Minimum GPA of 3.0 recommended for MSc and MBA programs.
Language Proficiency Required English Programs: IELTS 6.0+ / TOEFL iBT 80+ / Duolingo 105+
French Programs: DELF B2 / TCF B2 or equivalent French certification
Required Application Documents Academic transcripts (original & translated), updated CV/Resume, statement of motivation (500-800 words), two letters of recommendation, copy of passport, and proof of language proficiency.
Selection Process File evaluation followed by an online admissions interview. Decisions are typically communicated within 2-4 weeks of application completion. Rolling admissions — apply early for best scholarship consideration.
