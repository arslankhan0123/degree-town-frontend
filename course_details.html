Overview

The Bachelor in International Management at Clermont School of Business is a 3-year undergraduate program designed to build strong foundations in global business, management, marketing, and entrepreneurship. The program focuses on international exposure, practical learning, and cross-cultural business understanding, preparing students for global careers or further studies.
