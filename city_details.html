Transport & Connective
Metro system
Paris Metro covers 16 lines with 300+ stations. It is the quickest way to traverse the city, operating from 5:30 AM to 1:15 AM.
Train networks
RER express trains connect Paris to suburbs like CDG airport, Versailles, and Disneyland. TGV high speed links reach other cities.
Bike sharing
Vélib' Métropole is a massive bike rental scheme with 20,000+ docks. Student subscription discounts make it highly accessible.
Airports connectivity
Charles de Gaulle (CDG) and Orly (ORY) are the main hubs. Reached easily by RER B or airport shuttle lines from the city center.
