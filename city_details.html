Transport & Accessibility
Metro system
Paris Metro covers 16 lines with 300+ stations. Operating 5:30 AM – 1:15 AM. Student pass: €84/mo.
RER & Train
RER connects suburbs, CDG airport, Versailles, Disneyland. TGV high-speed links reach other cities.
Bike sharing
Vélib' Métropole has 20,000+ docks. Student discount subscriptions at just €3.10/mo.
Airports
CDG and Orly airports. Reached easily by RER B or shuttle from city center in ~30-45 mins.

Why / Why Not
Why Study in Paris?
  • World-class universities & research institutions
  • Rich culture, art, & history
  • Excellent public transport network
  • Vibrant student life & nightlife
  • Gateway to all of Europe
  • Affordable tuition (public unis from €170/yr)
Why Not?
  • Very high cost of living & rent
  • French language required for many programs
  • Crowded & fast-paced city lifestyle
  • Bureaucracy for visa & admin processes
  • Limited housing availability near universities
